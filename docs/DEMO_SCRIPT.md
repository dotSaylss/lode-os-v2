# LodeOS — 3-Minute Demo Script

*Read the **SAY** lines aloud. Do the **DO** lines. That's it. Target: under 3:00.
Setup, OBS settings, and deployment live in [DEMO_PRODUCTION.md](./DEMO_PRODUCTION.md).*

**Start state:** June Freedom workspace, fresh browser, first-run intro showing.

---

### COLD OPEN — 0:00–0:15

**SAY:** "An artist's money lives on six platforms. Nobody watches the gaps between them. LodeOS is the reasoning layer that does."

**DO:** Click through the intro — **Continue → Enter workspace** — landing on the chat home.

---

### ACT 1 · JUNE, THE ARTIST — 0:15–1:05
*Find the money you're owed.*

**DO:** In the chat, type **"What did I earn this year?"** → send. Wait for the answer.

**SAY:** "Simple questions hit a fast Flash front line." *(point at the caption: `Quick lookup · Gemini 2.5 Flash`)*

**DO:** Type **"Audit my royalties and draft the SoundExchange registration to recover what I'm owed."** → send. Wait for the trace and answer.

**SAY:** "Anything that needs real analysis or action is handed to a Gemini 2.5 Pro specialist — and it shows its work: what it read, who it consulted, which tier answered." *(point at the caption: `Reasoning · Gemini 2.5 Pro` and the trace chip `Consulted the royalty orchestrator`)*

**DO:** Click the **Open Today** card on the answer.

**SAY:** "Twenty-four hundred dollars in unclaimed neighboring rights — with the SoundExchange registration already drafted." *(point at the $2,400.00 card and the **Recover** button)*

**DO:** Click **Connectors** → on **SoundExchange** click **+ Connect**.

**SAY:** "And the access to go collect it — granted on her terms. Reads allowed; anything that changes her rights needs her approval." *(point at the scopes: Read → Allowed, Claim → Needs approval)*

**DO:** Click **Authorize SoundExchange** → let it land on "Connected as June Freedom."

---

### ACT 2 · LODE RECORDS, THE LABEL — 1:05–1:50
*Operate the whole roster.*

**DO:** Click the **account button** (bottom of rail) → **Lode Records**. Catalog appears.

**SAY:** "At the label, missing money compounds. Four hundred forty-six thousand dollars uncollected across fifty artists — auditing that by hand never happens."

**DO:** Click **Bulk register \<N> artists**. Let the agent trace render fully.

**SAY:** "One strategist agent reasons over the whole catalog — then hands off, agent to agent, to an execution agent that drafts every registration in one batch." *(point at the `LabelAgent → ActionAgent handoff` line as it appears)*

**SAY:** "Catalog ops that used to be a quarter's work."

---

### ACT 3 · KAI RIVERS, THE AI-NATIVE CREATOR — 1:50–2:45
*New revenue, on your terms.*

**DO:** Account button → **Kai Rivers**. Go to **Today**.

**SAY:** "Kai creates with Suno, keeps his library in Untitled, owns in Mogul, monetizes through Disco. A whole catalog, zero royalty infrastructure — until now." *(point at the playlists: Sync Ready, New Drops, Quiet Hours)*

**DO:** Hover the **"Glasslands"** row → click the **pitch button** (send arrow). The orb drafts a pitch.

**SAY:** "One click moves a track from playlist to paycheck — the sync dealmaker reads the live Disco briefs, matches on the track's real sound profile, and drafts the pitch for approval."

**DO:** In the orb, type **"Use Afterburn from my Sync Ready playlist and draft a Disco pitch for the best brief."** → send. Let the cross-connector trace render.

**SAY:** "His platforms don't talk to each other. Lode's agents talk to all of them — one ask carries a named track, from a named playlist, into a drafted pitch." *(point at the trace: Read Disco permissions → Loaded Disco briefs → Read Untitled library)*

**DO:** Go to **Connectors → Disco**, toggle **Draft pitches OFF**. Return to the orb, send the **same ask** again.

**SAY:** "And it's on a leash he sets. Turn the capability off, and the same agent refuses — and tells him why. Agency you can actually trust." *(the agent declines, citing the Disco setting)*

---

### CLOSE — 2:45–3:00

**SAY:** "Recover what you're owed. Operate at scale. Make new money — on your terms. LodeOS: the agentic control plane for the music business."

**DO:** Show the end card — *built on Google ADK · MCP · A2A · Gemini on Vertex AI · Cloud Run.*

---

*If you fall behind: cut Act 1's first question (the Flash lookup) and open straight on "Where am I losing money?" — you lose 12 seconds and keep the tier contrast.*
